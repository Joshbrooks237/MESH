What is Rick Simpson Oil?

Rick Simpson Oil (RSO) is a powerful, full-spectrum cannabis extract created by Canadian engineer Rick Simpson. After discovering its healing potential when treating his own skin cancer, Rick developed a method to extract the beneficial compounds from cannabis plants.1

Unlike many modern cannabis products, RSO contains the full range of cannabinoids, terpenes, and other beneficial compounds found in the cannabis plant. This "entourage effect" is what makes RSO so effective for many people.2

Key Facts About RSO:

  • Contains THC, CBD, and over 60 other cannabinoids3
  • Made from high-THC indica strains
  • Oil-based for better absorption
  • Used by millions worldwide

Important Safety Information

Start Low and Go Slow

RSO is potent and should be used responsibly. Always start with a very small dose and gradually increase as needed.

Consult Healthcare Professionals

While RSO has helped many people, it's important to consult with healthcare professionals, especially if you:

  • Are pregnant or breastfeeding
  • Have heart conditions
  • Take prescription medications
  • Have a history of mental health conditions

Quality Matters

Not all cannabis extracts are created equal. Look for high-quality, lab-tested RSO from reputable sources.
